India is a vast country with people belonging to different religions, languages, taste, preferences but the one thing that unites this county together is Cricket. MPL blends students' passion for Management and Cricket through an innovative format.

This one of a kind sporting league’s aim is to:

>> Focus on sports management as an industry through MPL
>> Test business knowledge as well as sporting skills in a single event
>> Create a networking and idea exchange platform for B-schools
>> Engage multiple skills and challenges to create a sense of teamspirit camaraderie.
>> Provide intellectual simulations along with spreading a message of individual health and well-being.

About Us




In 2006, IEIBS Akademia initiated the idea of supporting Stakeholders (Corporates, Institutes, Students Government) of The MBA industry. Over the years Akademia has supported 300+ students yearly along with our 5 associations (Partner Institutes) all over Maharashtra. The knowledge partner to these five Institutes, Akademia specializes in transforming young aspirants into world leaders. IEIBS Akademia-ASBS stands as the sole representative from India that has become an institutional member of CEEMAN. Mr. Nilesh Sarawate (CEO IEIBS Akademia) has been the chief protagonist in the concept and has mentored many students, Anmol Ambani (S/O'Anil Ambani) being one of the many, throughout his career & continues to do so. With mentors like Mrs. Munira Lokhandwala (A five-time CAT Topper & an Alumni of IIM Kolkata), IEIBS Akademia is assisting the aspirants to discover their potential and achieve a successful career.Akademia has initiated yet another concept through MPL by creating a stage for aspirants to showcase their talent in sports and management, providing them with an opportunity to take their skills to the corporate level.






The MPL Format

∙ 40+ B-schools participating in the Case Study Elimination Round
∙ Top 16 B-Schools participating in the Premier League
∙ 4 groups with 4 teams each.
∙ All Initial matches will be played using the regular league format.
∙ 3 league matches for each team.
∙ All matches of 6 overs each, with final match of 8 overs.
∙ Awards and recognition in all the matches.
∙ Attractive trophies with cash prizes.
